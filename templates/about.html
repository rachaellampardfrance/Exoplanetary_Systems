All data is gathered from NASA's Exoplanet Archive.

Additionally planetary data is gathered from the 'Planetary Systems Composite Data' (PSCompPars) table
comprised of data from 'Planetary Systems' (PS) table and may not be self consisent as data is
drawn from multiple sources. Data should not be used for acurate measurements but for hobby/interest.

Note that not all information may be Acurate as data is updated monthly and additionally handled before being represented on the site.

All figures are generated for this website and may also display inaccuracies.
